{{- /* Six principles grid */ -}}
01
Provenance over plausibility

Every finding links to a source. Every claim is falsifiable. We will never ship summary prose you cannot verify.

02
Constructs are first-class

Constructs are the atoms of reasoning. Shared across paxes, they become bridges. Agents use that to synthesize without glue code.

03
Findings carry uncertainty

Effect sizes, confidence intervals, p-values, sample sizes — not just direction. Strength labels are policed by editors, not by hope.

04
Open by default

CC-BY-4.0 for content. MIT for runtime. The registry is open. Forks are blessed. Mirrors are encouraged.

05
Composable, not monolithic

Paxes are YAML folders, not databases. No lock-in. Install what you need, compose at the construct level, run where you want.

06
Built for agents

Praxis MCP server. Tools for list, search, get findings, run playbooks. Agents can reason over your paxes like a research assistant.
